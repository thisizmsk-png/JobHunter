# Iron Temple -- 12-Week Training Log

> Print this log or use on a tablet. Fill in Weight x Reps @RIR for each set.
> Double progression: hit top of rep range on ALL sets, then increase weight.

---

## How to Use This Log

- **Weight x Reps @RIR** -- e.g., `185x8 @3` means 185 lbs for 8 reps with 3 reps in reserve
- **Myo-rep set** -- Do 12-15 activation reps, rest 5 breaths, do 3-5 mini-sets of 3-5 reps
- Leave Notes for form cues, pain, adjustments, or "top of range -- increase next week"

---

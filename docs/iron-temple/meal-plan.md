# Iron Temple -- 7-Day Meal Rotation

> Indian-American hybrid nutrition for lean bulk. 180-200g protein daily.
> Training days: 2600-2900 kcal | Rest days: 2400-2600 kcal
> No beef. No pork. Chicken, eggs, fish, goat only.

---

## How to Use This Plan

### Training Day vs Rest Day Calorie Cycling

Training days (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri) run at 2600-2900 kcal with higher carbs to fuel workouts and recovery. Rest days (Wed/Sat/Sun) drop to 2400-2600 kcal by reducing carbs -- protein stays at 180-200g every day. This calorie cycling approach partitions nutrients more efficiently: extra carbs go toward performance and glycogen replenishment on days you need them, while rest days keep a moderate surplus without unnecessary calorie spillover.

### How to Swap Meals Between Days

- Any Indian lunch can swap with another Indian lunch (e.g., swap chicken tikka + rice for fish curry + rice).
- Any American breakfast can swap with another American breakfast (e.g., swap egg scramble for oats + whey).
- Pre-workout and post-workout meals are interchangeable across training days as long as the macro profile is similar (30-40g P, 40-60g C pre; 40-50g P, 40-80g C post).
- Keep the daily protein total at 180-200g. If you swap a higher-protein meal out, compensate elsewhere.
- Rest day dinners are lower-carb. Do not swap a training-day high-carb dinner into a rest day without adjusting.

### Portion Adjustment

- **Gaining too fast (>2 lbs/month):** Remove 1/2 cup rice or 1 roti from lunch and dinner on training days. This cuts ~150-200 kcal. Reassess after 2 weeks.
- **Not gaining at all:** Add 1/2 cup rice to lunch and a banana + 1 tbsp peanut butter as an extra snack on training days. This adds ~250-300 kcal. Reassess after 2 weeks.
- **Protein feels low:** Add a Greek yogurt serving (23g P) between meals, or increase chicken portion by 50g (add ~17g P).

## Quick Reference: Pre/Post Workout Protocol

| Window | Timing | What to Eat | Target Macros |
|--------|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Pre-workout | 60-90 min before gym | Moderate protein + fast carbs, low fat | 25-40g P, 25-60g C, <10g F |
| Post-workout | Within 2 hours after gym | High protein + carbs for glycogen | 40-60g P, 40-80g C |
| Pre-bed | 30-60 min before sleep | Slow-digesting protein | 20-30g P (casein, cottage cheese, paneer) |

**Pre-workout options (interchangeable):**
- Whey shake + banana (fastest absorption)
- 1 roti + 100g chicken tikka (whole food option)
- Greek yogurt + honey + banana (if 90 min before)

**Post-workout options (interchangeable):**
- Tandoori chicken + rice + sabzi (Indian)
- Grilled chicken + sweet potato + broccoli (American)
- Goat keema + rotis + spinach (Indian)
- Salmon + rice + dal (Hybrid)
